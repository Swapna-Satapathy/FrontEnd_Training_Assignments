QUESTIONS ? NEED MORE INFORMATION ?

  • Overseas visitors can find a lot of information about Australian jumping standards, ratings, and equipment requirements in the Visitors and non-APF jumpers section.
    We have tried to answer general questions about on the FAQ Page. More questions can be answered through your local Drop zone or Club. Information about how the APF and skydiving got started can be answered by Clicking Here.
  • Technical questions can be directed towards the APF Office.
  • APF Office Staff also deal with licensing and rating questions.
  • Questions about APF Board decisions should be directed towards the Board Chair, or the Executive Assistant. Both are able to direct you to the appropriate Director if more information is required.
    If you are not sure who you need to talk to just contact the APF Office and we will direct you to the appropriate person.
  • Matters regarding this website (e.g. equipment for sale, changed contact details for clubs, etc.) can be directed to the APF Office.
